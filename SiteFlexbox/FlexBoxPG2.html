Unoffisial Elder Scrolls Pages

A nonprofit, ad-free wiki dedicated to The Elder Scrolls series, known for its accuracy, depth, and clean design. UESP is the gold standard for Elder Scrolls purists, while Fandom dominates casual searches. Many fans use both but prioritize UESP for serious research.

Praises: Ad-Free & Clean Interface, Nonprofit & Independent, Highly Detailed & Accurate and Better for Modders & Hardcore Fans. Criticism: Less Beginner-Friendly - "Articles can be overly technical, lacking the casual tone or visual appeal of Fandom", Slower Updates for New Content, Smaller Community, Fewer images\videos compared to fandom for visual aids,

Highly Detailed & Accurate - More technical and detailed mechanics with Highly curated lore and cited sources with less theoris, very tecnical information better for people with some knowledge about the series, Fewer images\videos compared to fandom for visual aids and has a complete and detayled world map for all of the game series.
